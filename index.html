Jose Barba-Montoya

I am an evolutionary biologist studying patterns of molecular evolution and diversification of plants and animals. My research combines phylogenetics/phylogenomics with systematics and ecology, and uses evolutionary frameworks to infer times of species divergences to establish a timescale for the tree of life, which is necessary for estimating the rates at which genes and genomes change .
